Who Are We?

BPM is a production company founded by Ethan J. Leon, in 2020 with his two companions Reuben Joseph and Carter Elcock. Their goal is to bring quality music and visuals to their island home St. Lucia. Coming from a musical background Ethan took over the Music aspect with Reuben and Carter handling the video and Marketing areas respectively.

Ethan Leon

Born and Raised in St. Lucia, Ethan took an early interest in music. At the age of 8 he was enrolled into the Saint Lucia School of Music. His love for music grew not only on his trumpet but also on the piano. "I spent many hours at the piano before and after my classes, my instructor would sometimes joke with me that I play the piano more than the trumpet," he said.

Carter Elcock

Carter Elcock was born with a mind for finance, from a young age he would go to school with an assortment of candies to sell to his peers. Although he doesn't know how to play or produce music he knows what good music sounds like and always brings something extra to the room. His role as a marketer for BPM is essential.

Reuben Joseph

Reuben Joseph grew up less than five minutes away from Ethan Leon. His interest in music began when he saw Ethan's growth, he enrolled at the SLSM where he learned guitar, but it was photography that captured his heart. He was the co-founder of the photography group "6teen." His love for photography extended to its close relative, cinematography. He is now our head of cinematography.

Our Mission

We want to augment the level of music and everything around it in St. Lucia, and leave a lasting mark on the world.
